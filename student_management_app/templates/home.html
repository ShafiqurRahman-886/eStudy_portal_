Welcome to E-Study Portal

E-Study Portal is created to help schools, instructors, and students to better share & communicate virtually in real-time.

Why you should use E-Study Portal?

Innovative Technologies

E-Study Portal is a Highly Flexible, Configurable, and Feature-rich solution as it is built using innovative technologies like Django, HTML, CSS, JavaScript; and to provide high availability, it was deployed on the Cloud platform.

Free to Use

E-Study Portal is free to use to provide freedom to access, study and share the learning contents whenever you like. It is a cost-effective solution to learn online as it does not require additional software.


24x7 Access

E-Study Portal platform is up and running 24x7 so that no barriers can stop one in their personal growth. It helps students, staff, and school admins to access the services at any time.



Developer Support

At the e-Study Portal, we provide you with support from our developer team in case of errors. Our Developer Team will try to connect and resolve the issue as soon as it is raised.
Therefore, you can use our services with ease.

Frequently Asked Questions


How do you get your Login Credentials?

Once your registration process is completed successfully, our school admin will share your ID and Password with you.

How can you be an instructor?

To be an instructor at our school, share your complete details using the contact page.

How can I apply for a leave?

Apply your leave of specific days with dates and a valid reason using your dashboard. Once the leave is approved, you are good to go.

Have errors while running the portal?

Raise your concern or query via the contact us page, and our developer team will be in touch with you soon.
